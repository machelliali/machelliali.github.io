About The Mantse

Our esteemed traditional leader serves as the custodian of our rich cultural heritage and the bridge between our ancestral traditions and modern progress.

With decades of service to our community, the Chief has been instrumental in preserving our cultural identity while leading initiatives that bring development and prosperity to our people.

Through wisdom, compassion, and dedication, our Chief continues to guide our community toward a brighter future while honoring the legacy of those who came before.

National Health Insurance Scheme Registration Program

Overview: A comprehensive community health initiative designed to ensure that every resident of Nii Boiman has access to quality healthcare through the National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS).

Program Details: Under the Chief's leadership, a free registration campaign was launched to help community members enroll in NHIS without financial barriers. The program provided:

  • Free registration services for all residents
  • Educational sessions on healthcare benefits and coverage
  • Assistance with documentation and enrollment process
  • Follow-up support to ensure smooth access to healthcare facilities

Impact: This initiative has significantly improved healthcare accessibility and affordability for the Nii Boiman community, ensuring that economic constraints do not prevent residents from accessing essential medical services.
